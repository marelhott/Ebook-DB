# Product Priorities

## True Order of Importance

1. `Library OS`
2. `Wireless Delivery`
3. `Translation Layer`
4. `AI Author & Book Intelligence`
5. `AI Recommendation Engine`

## Practical Meaning

This product is not primarily a desktop reading app.

It is primarily:

- a clean home for owned books
- a wireless bridge to real reading devices
- a translation workflow for long-form study
- an intelligence layer around authors and books
- a recommendation engine with explicit reasons

## Guardrails

- do not let reader polish dominate the roadmap
- do not let AI chat displace structured intelligence
- do not ship recommendations without reasons
- do not make delivery status opaque
- do not treat translation like a toy feature
